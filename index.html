Bio

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Angela Dai’s 3D AI Lab at the TU Munich. I received my master's degree in 2022 from ShanghaiTech University, working with Prof. Jingyi Yu in the Visual Intelligent Center (VIC). Before that, I received my bachelor's degree in 2019 from Shandong University. I was fortunate to have worked with Prof. Hao Su, Prof. Xuming He, and Prof. Lan Xu.

My research interest lies in 3D Computer Vision, Generative Models, Computer Graphics.
